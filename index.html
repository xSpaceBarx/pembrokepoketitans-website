Why Join Pembroke PokéTitans?

🤝 Friendly Community

Whether you're Level 5 or Level 80, everyone is welcome.

⚔ Weekly Meetups

Join us every Wednesday for Raid Hour and every major Pokémon GO event.

📍 Great Location

We meet at the Pembroke Historical Society Museum with five gyms within reach. 3 gyms a 4 minute walk to the center plaza and 3 gyms a 5 minute walk to the Herring Run.

🎉 Family Friendly

Players of all ages regularly attend our events. Ages 13+ encouraged to download and join!

Meet the Community

Pembroke PokéTitans brings together Trainers from across the South Shore for Raid Hours, Community Days, GO Fest, and special events. Whether you're returning to the game or chasing your next shiny, you'll find a welcoming group ready to play together.

Join the Community

Join our Campfire group to stay updated on meetups, raids, and special events.
